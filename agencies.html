Social Media Agencies

Analysis of leading social media marketing agencies and their approaches

SS

Social Shepherd

The results-driven Social first agency you've been looking for

What they do

They provide comprehensive social media marketing services with a focus on both organic and paid strategies. They develop bespoke strategies tailored to each client's specific needs and business objectives, taking a holistic approach that combines creative content with data-driven insights.

How they do it

Social Shepherd utilizes a strategic process that begins with comprehensive research and analysis of the client's industry, target audience, and competitors. They employ social listening tools to monitor conversations and trends, analytics platforms to track performance, and content creation tools to produce engaging social media content. Their approach emphasizes community nurturing and data-backed decision making, ensuring campaigns are both creative and effective.

Who works there

A team of social media strategists, content creators, account managers, data analysts, and influencer marketing specialists.

Who their clients are

A diverse range of clients across multiple industries, with a particular experience in DTC (direct-to-consumer) brands and businesses focused on e-commerce.

Money

They do not publicly display their prices, but large-scale companies like social sphere usually make around 12k to 50k+ a month or 6 to 8 figure monthly.

Where they are

Recently relocated to Bath, UK. The agency appears to be a medium-sized operation with a focus on maintaining a strong company culture and collaborative work environment.

Results

They measure their success and results through a combination of metrics that include engagement rates, follower growth, conversion rates, lead generation, and ROI. Following a transparent report.

What makes them successful

Social Shepherd builds trust through their comprehensive approach that combines creative excellence with strategic thinking, like Integrated Services, Creative Expertise, and Transparent Reporting.

Their niche

Is social-first marketing that combines creative content with strategic planning across organic and paid channels. They appear to specialize in comprehensive social media management for businesses, DTC brands, and e-commerce businesses.

VM

Voy Media

Creative Studio - Compelling content on the world's most active platforms

What they do

Voy's main services include full-service Facebook and Instagram advertising, retargeting campaigns, creative studio services, custom audience targeting, dynamic product ads, and real-time analytics.

How they do it

Voy Media follows a structured six-step process that begins with consultation and moves through account setup, creative research, campaign development, production, launch, and ongoing optimization. Their methodology includes A/B testing of ad copies, images, and targeting parameters, dynamic product ads for e-commerce clients, and multidimensional optimization that may include email marketing integration and landing page redesigns.

Who works there

A more diverse team structure that includes dedicated account managers, lead strategists, copywriters, graphic designers, illustrators, video editors, production specialists, and data analysts.

Who their clients are

Voy Media has clients that include Fortune 500 companies, e-commerce businesses, education providers, mobile apps, and startups.

Money

They do not publicly display their prices, but large-scale companies like social sphere usually make around 12k to 50k+ a month or 6 to 8 figure monthly.

Where they are

They have multiple locations, but primary operations are located in New York City, where they can talk to clients face to face in one of their headquarters.

Results

Voy Media focuses heavily on quantifiable business outcomes rather than vanity metrics. They track direct revenue generated, return on ad spend (ROAS), customer acquisition costs, and overall sales growth.

What makes them successful

Voy Media earns client trust through their proven results and entrepreneurial experience, like Performance-Based Approach, Entrepreneurial Background, Transparent Communication, Comprehensive Service Offering.

Their niche

They have a specific niche for Facebook and Instagram advertising specialists, particularly for direct response and e-commerce customer acquisition.
